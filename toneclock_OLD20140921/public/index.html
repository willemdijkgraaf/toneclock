About Tone Clock

Version:

The aim of this application is to give the contemporary composer/improviser a simple tool to print tone clock sheets.

A printed tone clock sheet can be used to manually draw harmonies and melodies (tone rows).

Feel free to experiment with the preferences in order to create a sheet that suites your needs and requirements.

Kind regards, Wim Dijkgraaf
